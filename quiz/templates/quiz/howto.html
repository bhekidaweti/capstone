Welcome to the Flag Quiz Game!

The Flag Quiz Game is an exciting and educational game that tests your knowledge of world flags. In this game, you'll be presented with a series of questions where you'll need to match the displayed country name with its corresponding flag. Sounds fun, right? To get started, simply click the "Start the Quiz" button. Once you begin, you'll see a country name displayed at the top, along with four flag images below it. Your task is to select the flag that represents the displayed country name. Click on the flag that you think matches the country name correctly. But here's the catch – you only have a limited amount of time to answer each question! Keep an eye on the countdown timer displayed in the top right corner of the screen. If you run out of time before making a selection, don't worry, you can always try again. For every correct answer, you'll earn points and move on to the next question. But be careful – if you choose the wrong flag, you'll need to try again. Your score will be displayed at the top left corner of the screen, so keep track of how well you're doing! Once you've answered all the questions or the time runs out, you'll see your final score. If you want to play again, simply click the "Restart" button to start a new quiz. If you're ready to continue your quiz journey, click "Continue" to load the next set of questions. So, are you ready to put your flag knowledge to the test? Let's play the Flag Quiz Game and see how many flags you can match correctly!
